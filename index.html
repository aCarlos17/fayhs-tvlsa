Welcome to Flora A. Ylagan High School

Thank you for choosing this school for your Senior High School journey.

Researchers from Flora A. Ylagan High School have created this self-assessment test to help you, the upcoming Senior High School students on choosing what strand is right for you from the available Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) strands that we offer here in Flora A. Ylagan High School. By taking this self-assessment test, TVL Strand Assessment will recommend you a strand based on your current knowledge, skills, and competencies.

Strands That We Offer

Here are the strands that are offered here in Flora A. Ylagan High School that are under the Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) track

Home Economics

Cookery

This strand will equip you the knowledge and skills that you must possess inside the kitchen before and whilst performing hands-on food preparation.
Home Economics

Tourism Promotion Services

This strand will equip you with knowledge and skills to operate an automated information system, source and provide destination information and advice, access and interpret product information, and promote tourism products and services.
Information Communications Technology (ICT)

Animation

This strand will equip you with knowledge and skills to produce clean-up and in-between drawings for their work to come to life.
Information Communications Technology (ICT)

Programming

This strand will equip you with knowledge and skills to the fundamentals of programming with VB.NET and Java, and learn HTML to create websites.
